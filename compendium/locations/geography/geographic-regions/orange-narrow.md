# Orange Narrow
Geographic region
{: .cap}

The **Orange Narrow** is the [Orange Gorge](/compendium/locations/orange-gorge/)'s narrow inner stretch. The [Primordial](/compendium/creatures/primordial/) formed it during [Late Genesis](/compendium/events/genesis/#late-genesis) in [432 million BW](/compendium/events/genesis/#432-million-bw) with its canyon. It connects to the [Orange Opening](/compendium/locations/orange-opening/).
